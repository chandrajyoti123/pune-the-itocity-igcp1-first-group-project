Cerebrum IT Park, Kalyani Nagar, Pune

Cerebrum is spread over 1 million square feet in the heart of the city, Kalyani Nagar. Cerebrum is a mere 5 km from the airport, 3 km from the railway station and a stone's throw from premium hotels. Cerebrum has been built by Kumar Builders, an ISO 9001:2000 accredited organisation. The park also offers unlimited data connectivity supported by STPI, uninterrupted power supply, broadband optical fibre network and video conferencing facilities for data transfer. The add-on facilities include ample parking area, a futuristic security system and an aesthetically designed common reception lobby - all in a beautifully landscaped area.
