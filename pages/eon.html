EON Free Zone is the epicentre of a growth-driven future that contributes to the dynamics of free- market democracy. A significant node and the first Special Economic Zone (SEZ) of Pune is located in Kharadi and spans a total area of ~ 6.2 million sq. ft. across Phase I & II. The campus spreads across ~57 acres and is the most prestigious IT destination within Pune's Eastern Corridor. EON Free Zone Phase I & II, with its innovative design, brings freedom-work culture alive. Phase I has a total built-up area of ~ 4 million sq. ft. and consists of interconnected five clusters, namely A, B, C, D and E. These clusters are well equipped with an array of amenities, making it more than a workplace. Eon Free Zone I is a foundation for the convergence of people, ideas, business, innovation and intelligence to create a thoughtful community. Eon Free Zone Phase II is built on the same principles, ideologies and pioneering qualities of Phase I. Sprawling over ~ 2.2 million sq. ft., Phase II with state-of-the-art infrastructure has a contemporary architecture style that provides easy interconnectivity. Phases I and II are fully operational and contribute immensely to the eastern business district of Pune.
