Weikfield IT Citi Info Park

Acquired in 2019, Weikfield IT Park is an eminent, high quality office park located in central Pune. Directly opposite the 300-key Hyatt Regency and adjacent to the 220-key Novotel hotel, the business park is three kilometres from Pune International Airport and six kilometres from Pune railway station. This highly sought after business destination is also a key residential hub with Kalyani Nagar and Koregaon Park a few minutes away, and has two large format, prominent retail malls in close proximity. Weikfield’s location and connectivity will be further enhanced with the completion of the Pune Metro in 2022, wherein the Metro station is proposed less than 500 meters from the park. The campus comprises three office buildings built on a 10-acre site, with a leasable area of 1.1 million square feet. Key clients include multinational corporations such as Maersk, Whirlpool, WNS, PTC, among others.
